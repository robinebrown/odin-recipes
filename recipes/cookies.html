Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

This recipe is for delicious chocolate chip cookies! They are the perfect treat for any occassion and are a fun way to get into baking!

    Ingredients
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking Soda
  • All-purpose Flour
  • Sugar
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Salt

Ready to Become a Bonafide Baker?

    Follow These Steps!
  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees (F)
  2. Combine 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour, 1 teaspoon baking soda and 1 teaspoon salt in a small mixing bowl.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, use an electric mixer to beat together 2 sticks of butter and 3/4 cups sugar until creamy.
  4. Then add your brown sugar and vanilla extract, and beat until thoroughly mixed.
  5. Working in that same bowl, add 2 eggs, one at a time, mixing after each egg is added. Continue mixing until creamy.
  6. Combine both bowls of mixed ingredients to form a single large, doughy mixture. Then, mix in chocolate chips as desired.
  7. Set aside cookie dough mixture. Cover cooking tray in non-stick spray, butter or parchment/wax paper.
  8. Use a common tablespoon to ball dough and place on baking tray. Leave 2-3 inches of empty space between dough balls on tray.
  9. Place baking tray in oven for 9 to 11 minutes, checking periodically and removing when cookies are golden brown.

All done! Enjoy your treat!
